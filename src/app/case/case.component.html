THE RESULTS

In collaboration with First National Bank, our team developed and executed a distribution strategy with engagement as the primary goal. This series was strategically hosted on First National Bank’s and Athlete Nation’s social media channels, with the video with the most social media likes receiving a $10,000 reward for the schools in that community. Grand Island, Nebraska was the recipient of this reward.

“WORKING WITH ATHLETE NATION TO DEVELOP AND EXECUTE OUR CHAMPIONS OF THE COMMUNITY EFFORT WAS SEAMLESS AND WE LOOK FORWARD TO CONTINUING OUR PARTNERSHIP TOGETHER.”
